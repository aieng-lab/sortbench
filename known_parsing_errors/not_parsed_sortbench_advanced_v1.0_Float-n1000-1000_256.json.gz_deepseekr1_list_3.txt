

To find the average of the given list of numbers:

1. Sum all the numbers: Add each element in the list together.
2. Count the total number of elements: Determine how many numbers are in the list.
3. Compute the average: Divide the total sum by the count.

The result will be the arithmetic mean of the dataset.
